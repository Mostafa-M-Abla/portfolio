Grammar Mistakes Detector

Detects if the given English sentence has grammatical mistakes.
The app uses a BERT model trained on the "The Corpus of Linguistic Acceptability (CoLA)" data set. The complete code repository is here.

Note: This app detects grammar mistakes, not spelling mistakes.
